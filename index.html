Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Quantum Reality Codes is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled.

Data Storage

All data — including your manifestation journal, streaks, and intention count — is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. No data is ever transmitted to any server. We have no database, no user accounts, and no way to access your intentions.

Minimal Analytics

Quantum Reality Codes uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand basic usage patterns such as page views and session counts. This helps us improve the app. GA4 may use cookies for this purpose. We do not use tracking pixels, retargeting, or any advertising trackers. Your intentions, journal entries, and personal content are never transmitted — they remain entirely on your device.

Progressive Web App

If you install Quantum Reality Codes as a PWA, it uses a service worker to cache assets for offline use. This is stored locally on your device and contains no personal information.

Third-Party Sharing

When you use the share feature, your reality code is shared through your device's native sharing or social media links. We do not intercept, store, or log any shared content.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
